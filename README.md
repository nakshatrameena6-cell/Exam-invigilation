# 📖 About SecureX

**SecureX** is an AI-powered online examination invigilation platform designed to enhance the integrity and security of remote examinations.

Traditional online exams heavily rely on human invigilators, making them prone to fatigue, inconsistency, and scalability issues. SecureX leverages **Computer Vision**, **Artificial Intelligence**, and **Real-Time Video Analytics** to automatically monitor candidates, detect suspicious activities, and generate comprehensive examination reports.

Rather than replacing human invigilators, SecureX acts as an intelligent assistant by continuously analyzing candidate behavior and providing real-time alerts along with post-exam evidence.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

With the rapid adoption of online education and remote hiring, ensuring fair examinations has become increasingly difficult.

Current challenges include:

- 📱 Mobile phone usage during exams
- 👥 Multiple people appearing in front of the camera
- 🚶 Candidate leaving the examination area
- 👀 Looking away from the screen
- 🤖 Manual monitoring becoming inefficient
- ⏳ Lack of real-time evidence generation
